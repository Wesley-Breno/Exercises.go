Use a list to solve the following problem. A company pays its salespeople based on commissions. The salesperson
receives $200 per week plus 9 percent of their gross sales for that week. For example, a salesperson who had gross sales of $3000 in a week receives $200 plus 9 percent of $3000, that is, a total of $470. Write a program (using an array of counters) that determines how many salespeople received salaries in the following ranges of values:

$200 - $299
$300 - $399
$400 - $499
$500 - $599
$600 - $699
$700 - $799
$800 - $899
$900 - $999
$1000 and above

Challenge: Create a formula to arrive at the position in the list based on the salary, without using multiple nested if statements.
